How to get a product link on Desktop or Laptop

Follow either method below — both take less than 10 seconds.

Method A

Using the Share button

Works on the Amazon website.

  1. 1
    Open the product page Search for the product you want and open its page. Make sure you are viewing the exact variant you want — colour, size, and model should be correct before copying the link.
  2. 2
    Find the Share button
  3. 3
    Click "Copy link"
  4. 4
    Paste the link into the input field in ProductLens using Ctrl + V (Windows) or Cmd+ V (Mac).
or
Method B

Using the address bar

The quickest way on a browser.

  1. 1
    Open the product page Search for the product you want and open its page. Make sure you are viewing the exact variant you want — colour, size, and model should be correct before copying the link.
  2. 2
    Click the address bar at the top of your browser.
  3. 3
    Copy the URL by pressing Ctrl + C (Windows) or Cmd + C (Mac).
  4. 4
    Paste the link into the input field in ProductLens using Ctrl + V (Windows) or Cmd + V (Mac).
💡 Tip: You can compare 2 to 5 products at once. Open each product in a separate browser tab, copy the URL from each, and paste them one by one into ProductLens.

How to get a product link on Mobile

Works with the Amazon app on both Android and iPhone.

1

Open the product in the Amazon app

Search for the product you want and open its page. Make sure you are viewing the exact variant you want — colour, size, and model should be correct before copying the link.

2

Tap the Share button

Look for the Share icon

3

Tap "Copy link"

A share sheet will appear with several options. Tap Copy link or Copy. The product link is now saved to your clipboard.

4

Paste the link into ProductLens

Open ProductLens in your mobile browser. Tap on an input field, then tap and hold to bring up the paste option, and tap Paste. Repeat this for each product you want to compare.

💡 Tip: Open each product in a separate Amazon app session, copy the link, switch to your browser, paste it in, then go back to the app for the next product.
